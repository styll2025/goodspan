What is a Season?

Thirty days devoted to one theme. Pick a level from Gentle to Deep, choose a few daily practices, and carry them for the month. Everyone starts and finishes together.

What is a Circle?

A small group of 5–7 people you haven’t met, all on the same Season. You share your practice, stay connected, and meet weekly. On day 30 every Circle gathers in person to close the Season.
